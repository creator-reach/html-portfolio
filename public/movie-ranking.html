Favourite Anime Watchlist By Papi

Top 5 Anime of all Bloody Time

1. One Piece

This honestly has to be one of the best animes of all time, if not the best.
Whether it is the Plot, the Characters and their various development throughout the story, whether its the world , the art or the animation. Each and everyone of those factors have been honed to the absolute peak of media. It has over 1500 episodes, yet it still has the captivating power to keep one engaged and not get tired even after binge watching for days one end. The best anime I tell you, I rate it a 1000000/100.

2. Naruto

Honestly, what else is there to say. This anime is not just a film, its a life lesson turned into an Epic story of how an enstranged boy bereaved of all his loved ones and outcasted by his entire village goes from being a notorious trouble maker to one of the most respected and powerful individuals on his planet.

3. Gintama

No More questions need to be brought forward for this anime. It is by far, historically and evidently the most funny anime in existence. But dont let the laughs and giggles distract you from the engaging storyline of how a lost ronin, an alien withe her dog and a samurai trainee work together to unravel the deeper mysteries plaguimng the current state of their country.

4. Attack on Titan

This anime was the turning point of all anime in the current century gaining worldwide attraction. It managed to use its deep engaging plot to attract new believers into the anime cult. One of the best animes of all time.

5. Dororo

Absolute Cinema. Peak writing.
